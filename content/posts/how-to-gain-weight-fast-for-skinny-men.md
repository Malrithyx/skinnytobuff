# How to Gain Weight Fast: A Guide for Skinny Men

If you've always been "the skinny guy," gaining weight can feel like an impossible task. You might feel like you eat "a ton," but the scale never moves. Here's the truth: you're simply not eating enough calories.

## The Science of Gaining Weight

To gain weight, you must be in a **Caloric Surplus**. This means consuming more calories than your body burns for energy and daily functions.

### Step 1: Calculate Your TDEE
Your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is how many calories you burn in a day. To gain weight, aim for 300-500 calories *above* your TDEE.

### Step 2: Focus on Calorie-Dense Foods
Don't fill up on salads. Focus on foods that pack a lot of calories into small volumes:
*   **Healthy Fats:** Avocado, olive oil, nuts, and seeds.
*   **Liquid Calories:** Homemade mass gainer shakes (Oats, peanut butter, milk, protein powder).
*   **Protein Sources:** Whole eggs, fatty fish, and red meat.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1.  **Skipping Meals:** Consistency is key. Aim for 4-5 smaller meals throughout the day.
2.  **Too Much Cardio:** Limit intense cardio sessions, as they burn the calories you need for growth.
3.  **Lack of Sleep:** Muscle is built while you sleep, not while you're at the gym. Aim for 7-9 hours.

## Recommended Supplements

While food should always come first, these can help:
*   **Creatine Monohydrate:** Increases strength and muscle volume.
*   **Whey Protein:** A convenient way to hit your protein goals.
*   **Casein Protein:** Great for a slow release of protein before bed.
