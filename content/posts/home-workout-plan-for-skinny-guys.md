# The Ultimate 30-Day Home Workout Plan for Skinny Guys

If you're a "hardgainer" or a skinny guy looking to pack on serious muscle, you don't need a fancy gym. You need a consistent plan that focuses on progressive overload and high-quality movement.

## Why Most Skinny Guys Fail to Build Muscle at Home

Most skinny guys make the mistake of doing too much cardio or high-volume isolation exercises. To grow, you need to focus on **compound movements** that recruit multiple muscle groups and trigger a hormonal response.

## The "Hypertrophy First" 3-Day Full Body Split

This routine is perfect for beginners because it allows for maximum recovery—which is when your muscles actually grow. If you're struggling to put on size, make sure you're following our [weight gain guide for skinny men](/blog/how-to-gain-weight-fast-for-skinny-men).

### Day 1: Foundation & Strength
1. **Pushups:** 3 sets of 10-15 reps (Scale to knee pushups if needed)
2. **Bodyweight Squats:** 3 sets of 20 reps
3. **Inverted Rows:** 3 sets of 8-10 reps (Use a sturdy table or low bar)
4. **Plank:** 3 sets, hold for 45 seconds

## Nutrition: You Can't Out-Train a Bad Diet

You cannot out-train a bad diet. If you want to build muscle, you **must** be in a caloric surplus. Aim for 0.8g to 1g of protein per pound of body weight.

### Example "Gainer" Meal Plan
* **Breakfast:** Oatmeal with peanut butter, protein powder, and a banana.
* **Lunch:** Chicken breast, brown rice, and broccoli.
* **Dinner:** Lean beef or salmon with sweet potatoes.
